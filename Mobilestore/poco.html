History and Background:

Launch: Poco was launched in August 2018 with the introduction of the Poco F1. It was originally positioned as a sub-brand of Xiaomi but has since operated more independently.

Brand Philosophy: Poco focuses on delivering high performance and advanced features at a value-for-money price, appealing to tech enthusiasts and budget-conscious consumers alike.

Product Lineup:

Poco F Series: Known for flagship-level performance at a lower price point. The Poco F1, for example, was notable for its high-end specifications at a relatively low cost.

Poco X Series: Aimed at providing a balance between performance and affordability, with features that cater to both gaming and everyday use.

Poco M Series: Designed for budget-conscious users, offering good performance and features at a more accessible price point.

Global Reach: Poco has expanded its market presence internationally, with availability in various regions outside of its home base in China.
